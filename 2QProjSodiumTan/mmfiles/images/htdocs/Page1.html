Mondria

  • Mondria is the initial setting for the characters thanks to its great power and stability
  • Though, among all nations, Mondria opposes the use of magic daily life, associating it with evil and chaos
  • Appropriately, they are the best suited against magical threats thanks to their ability to counter it
  • Enoch is a Black Cat Beastfolk, looking like an anthropomorphic black cat despite his second set of eyes in place of eyebrows
  • Early on in the story Enoch is revealed to be concealing his true appearance with glamour, which is discovered by the female protagonist, Teresa. He later assists them as thanks for keeping his identity secret
  • The Heroes make use of Enoch's ability "Sell Your Spirit" to find an assassin that was lurking in the imperial palace. Later proven to have worked because four large eyes began stalking the assassin from the dark

Trivia

  • Mondria's military strategy revolves around countering magic users using a material that absorbs and nullifies magic called Withersteel
  • One of the largest Mondrian merchant guilds is owned by Enoch, making him wealthy and powerful
  • Despite the anti-magic orientation of the country, Mondria is by far the largest, spanning around three continents
