The Guide To Adrala

  • Adrala is the novel's world or main setting which is broken up into separate nations each with a different quirk.
  • The world is supported with the typical magic system in most fantasy stories, in this case the magic originates from powerful concentrations called
    Leylines
  • Magic and science are competing forces in progress, the characters are the ones trying to seek this balance between whimsical and mundane
